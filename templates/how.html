Point system

What are swap points?

Swap Points are the points you earn for every item you hand in. The more points you collect, the more you can swap. Simply choose clothes with the equivalent point value and take them home. You can check your points in your digital app and use them at our events. Your points never expire.

How are points determined?

Every item starts with a base point value based on its type. Bonus points are then added depending on the item’s condition, brand, material, or whether it is brand new with tags.
