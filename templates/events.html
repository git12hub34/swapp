First time?

Before your first swap

Not been to a swap before? Here is exactly what to expect from the moment you arrive to finding your new pre-loved pieces.

1

Book a spot

Choose your event and reserve your place. You can also join the app for easier future events.

2

Bring items

Bring clean, wearable pieces. They can be clothes, accessories, shoes or other accepted items.

3

Get points

Your items are checked and converted into points. The exact value depends on condition and category.

4

Start swapping

Use your points to take home new pre-loved pieces from the swap floor.

Practical tips

What to know before arriving

Everything you need to know before you arrive — so your first event feels easy and stress-free.

What to bring

  • Clean clothes in good condition
  • Accessories, shoes, bags or other accepted items
  • Items you would be happy to give to someone else
  • Your phone for ticket, reminder or app check-in

Before you arrive

  • Check the address and public transport route
  • Arrive during the check-in period
  • Read the point system explanation
  • Bring fewer but better items if you are unsure

Map and accessibility

Location map, public transport options and accessibility information will appear here for each event.

Questions

Event questions

Can I join if I have never swapped before?

Yes. The event flow is made for first-time visitors too. You will check in, receive points and then start browsing.

Do I need to bring many items?

No. You can start with a small number of good-quality pieces. The point system is there to make the exchange feel fair.

What if I do not use all my points?

Remaining points can be saved and used again at another event through the app or membership system.

Can I bring items other than clothes?

Depending on the event, accessories, shoes, bags and other useful items may be accepted. Check the event page for the full accepted items list before you arrive.
