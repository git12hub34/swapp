About us

We started with one simple idea: people have so much to give and even more to gain when they swap, not shop.

Today, The Swap Club is a growing movement of people who come together to shop more sustainably with fashion, accessories, and much more. Whether you’re here to refresh your closet, make new friends, or simply try something new — everyone is welcome.
