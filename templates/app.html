Single ticket or membership?

Membership is useful for people who want to return regularly, while a single ticket is a simple first step.

Single ticket

  • Good for trying one event.
  • Pay per event.
  • Useful for first-time visitors.
  • Less connection with long-term swapping history.

Membership

  • Better for regular swappers.
  • Financially better over time.
  • Keep points and event reminders in one place.
  • Supports long-term community participation.
